About Me

I am a 3rd-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science, College of William and Mary. I am a part of LENS group, under supervision of Prof. Gang Zhou. I received my M.Eng. in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems in June 2010 from National Key Laboratory of Pattern Recognition at Institute of Automation, CAS, and my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science in June 2007 from Nanjing University.

My research interests are mobile and ubiquitous computing. Particularly, my current work includes the following topics:

      - Context-aware Bandwidth and Power Management on Smartphones
      - Effective & Efficient Context Recognition Systems
  I am currently looking for an internship starting from August 25th, 2013.
